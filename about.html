Davolee Travel & Tours is a vibrant and dynamic Nigerian owned private company set up in 2005 and has its head office at 7A Awudu Ekpekha Boulevard, off Admiralty Way, Lekki Phase 1, Lagos. We are ranked among the top Travel Management Companies in Nigeria.
We are a fully-fledged IATA approved Travel Agency and, a member of the National Association of Nigeria Travel Agencies (NANTA).
We specialize in Corporate Travel delivering time and cost efficiencies for large and small companies, and individuals.
We provide a wide range of services pertaining to the travel and tourism industry. Our services are very affordable with every depth of excellence attached to it so as to give our clients the best travel and tourism experience they can ever have.
